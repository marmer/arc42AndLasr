LASR — Overview

LASR — Core Review

  • Understand what makes you special (Lean Mission Statement)
  • Gather fundamental requirements (arc42 ch.1)
  • Weight the most important quality attributes
  • Explore the architecture
  • Risk-based review
  • Quality-focused analysis

LASR+ — Optional Deep Dive

  • Dive deeper into specific topics
  • Pearl diving — find hidden strengths
  • Environment exploration
  • Root-cause analysis methods

source: lasr-reviews.org/cheat-sheet

  • LASR core review: fast, targeted results
  • Understand what makes the system special; explore the architecture
  • LASR+ (optional deep dive — not today's focus): methods to get to the root of problems, dive for pearls, explore the environment
  • Primarily designed for small reviews, but also suitable for larger ones
  • Sometimes other methods are more appropriate (politics, complexity, review goal)
